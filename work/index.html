The AI Operations Audit

Two weeks, fixed fee. One 90-minute session where I watch how you actually work, then I audit nine areas of your operation and hand you a report you own outright.

What I look at — all nine

  • Inbox — response debt, what actually needs you
  • Files — where things live vs. where they're supposed to
  • Projects & clients — what fell through the cracks last quarter, and why
  • Money admin — invoicing, receipts, orphaned subscriptions
  • Inbound — time-to-first-response, where inquiries leak
  • Outbound — follow-up discipline, warm leads going cold
  • Marketing & content — actual cadence vs. intended
  • Existing automation — what's bolted on, documented, and understood
  • Build / buy / partner — what you own vs. rent, and whether you could fire a vendor without losing the function

What you get — the report

  1. Executive summary — your three biggest leaks, costed
  2. Current-state map — your operation as it actually runs, on one page
  3. Findings per area — graded, with the specific fix
  4. Quick wins — under-a-week fixes with instructions, yours either way
  5. Phased roadmap — with a do-it-yourself path and a done-for-you path, priced
  6. Build / buy / partner calls — per capability
  7. Drift forecast — where entropy comes back in 6–12 months

If you never spend another dollar with me, the report should still have been worth it. That's the bar.
